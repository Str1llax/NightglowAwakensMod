Nightglow Reimagined (English)

Nightglow Reimagined is my first mod created with love, for my best friend rain4ik.
Without his idea and inspiration, this mod would never have existed.
This is not just a balance tweak — it’s a full journey built around his favorite weapon.
Mod supports English en_US and Russian ru_RU languages.

What This Mod Does

This mod reworks Nightglow into a weapon you can enjoy from early game to endgame.
Whether you want full control or a guided progression experience, this mod adapts to your playstyle.

Designed for casual players, the weapon is strong, comfortable to use, and never punishing.

Config Options

The mod includes a configurable system with two gameplay modes:

1. Constant Damage Mode
Nightglow keeps a fixed base damage
You can manually set damage from 0 up to 2,147,483,646
Perfect for testing, sandbox play, or fun overpowered runs


2. Progression Mode
Find Nightglow in the early game on Floating Islands
Use the same weapon through the entire game
Nightglow grows stronger after defeating almost every boss
Damage is carefully balanced to stay fun and accessible


Progression & Tooltips

In Progression Mode, Nightglow evolves alongside your journey:
The weapon improves after boss defeats
Tooltip updates dynamically
Tooltips provide a little lore and tell you which boss to defeat next
Helps guide players naturally through Terraria progression

No guides, no wiki needed — the weapon itself becomes your compass.

Who Is This Mod For?
Players who love Nightglow and consider it too weak for its vanilla progression!
Casual players who want a smooth, enjoyable experience.
Those who want progression without extreme difficulty.

This mod is about fun, comfort, and flow — not punishment.

Настройки конфига

Мод включает настраиваемую систему с двумя режимами игры:

1. Режим Модификации Урона
Меняет Ночному Сиянию фиксированный базовый урон
Урон можно задать от 0 до 2 147 483 646
Идеально подходит для тестов, развлекушек или весёлых дисбалансных прохождений


2. Режим прогрессии
Найти Ночное Сияние можно в ранней игре на Летающих островах
Использовать одно и то же оружие на протяжении всей игры
Ночное Сияние становится сильнее после победы почти над каждым боссом
Урон сбалансирован так, чтобы оставаться доступным и весёлым (Сложно НЕ БУДЕТ!)


Прогрессия и подсказки

В режиме прогрессии Ночное Сияние развивается по мере прохождения игры:
Оружие улучшается после победы над боссами.
