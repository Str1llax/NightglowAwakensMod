[h1]Nightglow Reimagined EN[/h1]

[b]Nightglow Reimagined[/b] is my [b]first[/b] mod created with love, for my best friend [u]rain4ik[/u].
Without his idea and inspiration, this mod would never have existed.
This is not just a balance tweak — it’s a full journey built around his favorite weapon.
Mod supports English [code]en_US[/code] and Russian [code]ru_RU[/code] languages.

[h2]What This Mod Does[/h2]

This mod reworks [i]Nightglow[/i] into a weapon you can enjoy from early game to endgame.
Whether you want full control or a guided progression experience, this mod adapts to your playstyle.

Designed for [b]casual players[/b], the weapon is strong, comfortable to use, and never punishing.

[h3]Config Options[/h3]

The mod includes a configurable system with [b]two gameplay modes[/b]:

[b]1. Constant Damage Mode[/b]
[list]
    [*]Nightglow keeps a fixed base damage
    [*]You can manually set damage from [i]0 up to 2,147,483,646[/i]
    [*]Perfect for testing, sandbox play, or fun overpowered runs
[/list]

[b]2. Progression Mode[/b]
[list]
    [*]Find Nightglow in the [u]early game[/u] on Floating Islands
    [*]Use the same weapon through the entire game
    [*]Nightglow grows stronger after defeating almost every boss
    [*]Damage is carefully balanced to stay fun and accessible
[/list]

[h3]Progression & Tooltips[/h3]

In [b]Progression Mode[/b], Nightglow evolves alongside your journey:

[list]
    [*]The weapon improves after boss defeats
    [*]Tooltip updates dynamically
    [*]Tooltips provide a [i]little lore[/i] and [b]tell you which boss to defeat next[/b]
    [*]Helps guide players naturally through Terraria progression
[/list]

No guides, no wiki needed — the weapon itself becomes your compass.

[h2]Who Is This Mod For?[/h2]

[list]
    [*]Players who love Nightglow and consider it too weak for its vanilla progression!
    [*]Casual players who want a smooth, enjoyable experience.
    [*]Those who want progression without extreme difficulty.
[/list]

This mod is about [b]fun, comfort, and flow[/b] — not punishment.
